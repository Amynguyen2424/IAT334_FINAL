Walkthrough Of Our Prototype

Explore the key features of Shachi Rental in action

▶

Step 1

Welcome & Onboarding

Start your rental journey with a friendly introduction and simple account creation. The onboarding process is designed to be welcoming and straightforward, getting you started quickly without overwhelming details.

  • Friendly welcome message introducing the platform
  • Simple account creation with email or Google sign-up
  • Phone and face verification for credibility
▶

Step 2

Customize Your Search

The platform shows you exactly how well each property matches your criteria, so you can focus on places that actually fit your needs.

  • Interactive price range slider with market data visualization
  • Specify bedroom and bathroom requirements.
  • Filter by property type: apartment, townhouse, or entire place.
▶

Step 3

Discover Your Options

Browse on an interactive map or scroll through a personalized feed. Get instant insights about neighborhoods without opening multiple tabs or juggling different websites.

  • Interactive map with price indicators.
  • Property cards showing key details at a glance.
  • Neighborhood insights, transit access, and average pricing.
▶

Step 4

Get the Full Picture

Every listing gives you the complete picture to make informed decisions. No more piecing together information from scattered sources or wondering what you're missing.

  • Detailed lease information including move-in dates and terms.
  • Nearby transit, schools, and amenities on an integrated map.
  • Match percentage breakdown showing why this property fits you.
▶

Step 5

Keep Track of Favorites

Create custom collections to organize properties by neighborhood, price range, or any system that works for you. Share collections with potential roommates and compare properties side-by-side.

  • Save unlimited properties to custom-named folders.
  • Quick access to all saved listings and collections.
▶

Step 6

Connect with Landlords

Stay on top of all your applications without the guesswork. No more wondering where you stand or losing track of which properties you've applied to.

  • Direct messaging with landlords and property managers.
  • Schedule viewings with calendar integration (virtual or in-person).
  • Track all conversations and viewing appointments in one place.
▶

Step 7

Monitor Progress

Fill out your renter profile once, then apply to multiple homes with a few taps.

  • Sachi guides you through creating your outstanding applications.
  • Upload and manage verification documents securely.
  • Keeps your applications organized in one dashboard.
  • Notifications appear when status changes or landlords respond.
▶

Step 8

Stand Out as a Renter

Build a complete profile that shows landlords you're a serious applicant. Having everything ready upfront helps you move faster when you find the right place.

  • Having verification badges show landlords you're a good fit.
  • Complete renter profile with preferences and history.
  • Get personalized tips from Shachi to improve your profile.
