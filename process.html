1.

Understand The Problem

Metro Vancouver's rental market presents significant challenges that heavily impact newcomers and international students:

Less than 1% vacancy rate in Metro Vancouver

Duplicate listings across multiple platforms

Increasing rental scams targeting newcomers

Limited trust mechanisms between parties

Problem statement

How might we redesign the rental search process to prevent scams and optimize efficiency between verified landlord and renter matching?

2.

Project Goal

Vision & Objectives

Primary Goal

Create a mobile app that helps renters find verified listings, connect with legitimate landlords, manage applications efficiently, and understand their tenant rights, transforming an overwhelming process into a supportive, approachable experience.

3.

Our Solution

Unified Search Flow

Aggregate listings from multiple platforms and eliminate duplicates automatically, saving renters hours of browsing time.

Verification System

Trust scores and verification data help confirm legitimate landlords before applying, reducing the risk of scams.

One-Time Upload

Build your renter profile once with secure document storage and apply to multiple properties instantly.

Direct Communication

Chat with landlords, schedule viewings, and manage maintenance requests all in the app.

4.

Key Insights Gathered From Interviews

1.

Repetitive Documentation Wastes Time

Renters repeatedly upload the same documents across platforms. A centralized, reusable renter profile saves time and reduces frustration during applications.

2.

Verification Must Be Front and Center

International students lack local knowledge to spot scams. Badges, reviews, and trust scores need to be prominent on listings, not hidden.

3.

Keep the Supportive Tone Consistent

Our Shachi guide‚Äôs warm tone resonated during onboarding but faded later. It should stay present throughout flows to keep the supportive feel.

4.

Duplicate Listings Overwhelm Users

Multiple platforms show duplicates and outdated posts. Aggregating, deduplicating, and verifying listings builds confidence and saves time.

5.

Users Need Visual Match Indicators

Preference matches as text were confusing. Clear color-coded indicators and visuals make it obvious which properties fit.

6.

Application Progress Lacks Clarity

Applicants struggled to see status and next steps. Clear tracking and confirmations reduce stress throughout the process.
